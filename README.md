# 📖 Executive Summary

This project presents an interactive Excel dashboard designed to analyze the global data job market through salary trends, skill demand, remote work opportunities, and geographic salary distribution.

Using Power Query, Pivot Tables, Excel formulas, and dynamic visualizations, raw job posting data was transformed into a business intelligence dashboard that enables users to explore key workforce trends through interactive filters and KPI-driven insights.

The dashboard provides a comprehensive view of the market by highlighting median salaries across job roles, countries, and work types, identifying the most in-demand technical skills, comparing remote and on-site opportunities, and uncovering high-paying career paths within the data industry.

By converting complex job market data into clear visual insights, this project demonstrates how Excel can be used as a powerful analytical and decision-support tool for career planning, market research, and business intelligence applications.

# 🔍 Key Business Findings

## 💰 Salary Insights

- Senior Data Scientist is the highest-paying role in the dataset.
- Senior Data Scientists earn approximately **72% more** than Data Analysts.
- Salary levels vary significantly across different job categories.

📌 Highlights the impact of specialization and experience on compensation.

---

## 🌍 Global Market Coverage

- Job opportunities span **111 countries**.
- Salary distributions vary considerably across regions.
- Global demand for data professionals continues to grow.

📌 Demonstrates worldwide opportunities in data careers.

---

## 🏠 Remote Work Analysis

- Remote positions account for **12.38%** of total job postings.
- Both remote and on-site opportunities remain significant parts of the market.

📌 Useful for professionals evaluating flexible work options.

---

## 🛠 Skills Demand Analysis

- SQL and Python remain among the most requested skills.
- Cloud and BI technologies appear frequently in job requirements.
- Skill demand varies across job roles and locations.

📌 Helps identify high-value skills for career development.
